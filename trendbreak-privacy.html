Privacy Policy

TrendBreak does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

All calculations and analytics are performed locally on the user’s device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

The app may access publicly available market data from third-party providers. This data is used solely for informational and educational purposes.

TrendBreak does not require user accounts, login, or personal information.
