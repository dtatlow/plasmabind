PlasmaBind Inc.

PlasmaBind Inc. develops pharmacokinetic simulation software designed to help researchers and clinicians better understand medication exposure in complex patients.

Our work focuses on modeling protein binding, albumin dynamics, and drug distribution for highly protein-bound medications.
