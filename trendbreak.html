TrendBreak

Educational Market Analytics

TrendBreak is an educational market analytics application designed to visualize price instability, momentum, and signal patterns across stocks.

The app combines multiple dimensions of market behavior, including instability (EBI), momentum, and signal classifications such as breakout, compression, and exhaustion.

TrendBreak is intended for educational and research purposes only. It does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations, or trading instructions.

Market data may be delayed or incomplete. All outputs are informational only.
